A mind that designs,
a heart that writes

Kadeeja Dhiya is a multidisciplinary creative from Bengaluru, India — a graduate in both Fashion Design and English Literature. Her work occupies the beautiful intersection between aesthetic form and narrative meaning: collections that tell stories, and writing that dresses ideas in vivid colour.

Her academic journey — from the structured world of pattern-making to the expansive universe of literary theory — has shaped a creative perspective that is simultaneously rigorous and deeply imaginative. She brings both the precision of a designer and the empathy of a writer to every project she undertakes.

With an eye for silhouette and an ear for story, Kadeeja crafts work that resonates — whether on the runway or on the page.
